About Me

Hello, my name is Ben Martin. I am a student at George Mason University in Fairfax, VA obtaining my Bachelors of Science in Psychology. My interests include behavioral profiling, observation, data analysis, and all things mystery! My two main hobbies include reading and playing the strategy game Go. I enjoy reading whatever I can get my hands on but have recently been reading more psychology related books. The game Go interests me because of it’s seemingly simple rules but deeply complex strategy. I am always trying to find more people to play with!
