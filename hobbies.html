Hobbies

If you look under the Projects tab you may notice I have a lot, you could even say a hobby of mine is starting ideas but never finishing them! But I count Reading and Go as my two main hobbies.

Reading
I really enjoy reading, it gives me the chance to view a world through someone elses eyes. I'll admit, sometimes I get too into a book and tune the rest of the world out for a bit. My goal is to read every book someone reccomends to me along with my continusouly growing list of books!

Go/Baduk/Weiqi
I love this game! Go is an ancient strategy game, estimated to have been created 3,000-4,000 years ago. While the rules seem simple, the strategy goes deep. It is a really helpful game to learn how to plan, and need to make changes under time pressure. I'm currently playing on Online Go Server (OGS), my username is Tesuji Ibis. I look forward to any challenges, I'm still a relative beginner so I would love any advice!
